Loan2Save

About LOAN2SAVE Daily Contributions Program

The SOM LOAN2SAVE Daily Contributions program is a flexible, short-term loan facility designed to support our cooperative members, particularly traders with daily or weekly incomes. This unique savings plan offers members the ability to access loans directly linked to their daily or weekly contributions, providing an efficient and reliable financial solution for those with consistent earnings.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Subscriber Profile: Applicants must be active traders or individuals whose income is generated on a daily or weekly basis.
  • Business Requirement: The applicant must own or manage a business or shop where they conduct trade or provide services.
  • Maximum Loan Amount: The loan amount requested must not exceed N100,000, with the maximum daily contribution limited to N5,000.
  • Tenancy Verification: The loan officer must verify that the applicant's business location is legally occupied, with evidence of up-to-date rent payments.
  • Contribution Period: To qualify for a loan, applicants must have made the agreed daily contributions for a minimum of 30 consecutive days.
  • Loan Amount Eligibility: Eligible members can access loans up to double the total amount they have contributed, with the maximum loan tenure set at 30 days.

Loan Repayment Terms

  • Daily Repayment: Loans under the LOAN2SAVE program must be repaid on a daily basis. The loan officer will ensure strict adherence to repayment schedules, monitoring progress continuously.
  • Default Penalty: Any subscriber who defaults on repayment for three consecutive days will become ineligible for future loans, regardless of whether the outstanding balance has been settled.

Verification and Approval Process

Verification of Information: Loan officers are required to verify the applicant's and their guarantor's addresses and other provided information to ensure their accuracy and validity. Only after thorough verification will the loan application be processed.
