About Us

Who We Are

SOM Global Multipurpose Co-operative Society is a dynamic and forward-thinking co-operative committed to transforming lives and empowering communities. As a multi-purpose cooperative society, we are driven by a passion to deliver innovative, accessible, and affordable financial solutions tailored to the needs of individuals, small businesses, and growing enterprises.

Our vision is to become the leading name in the microfinance sector—an institution recognized not just for financial services, but for real impact. We believe in the power of financial inclusion as a catalyst for economic growth and sustainable development.

Backed by a team of experienced professionals and guided by the principles of integrity, transparency, and community development, SOM Global is more than a cooperative—we are a movement. A movement rooted in trust, driven by innovation, and committed to delivering exceptional service that adds lasting value to our members and stakeholders. At SOM Global, we don’t just offer financial solutions—we build futures, nurture dreams, and support the aspirations of those we serve.

Our Management

Our Vision

At SOM GLOBAL Multipurpose Co-operative Society, we aspire to become Nigeria’s foremost cooperative and microfinance institution—recognized for excellence, integrity, and innovation. Our vision is to empower individuals, especially low- and middle-income earners, by providing inclusive, affordable, and transformative financial solutions. Through unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction, financial literacy, and sustainable development, we aim to create lasting value and drive economic prosperity across communities—positioning SOM GLOBAL as the benchmark for trust and impact in Nigeria's microfinance sector.

Our Mission

Our mission is to drive inclusive economic empowerment by alleviating poverty and promoting financial stability among active low- and middle-income earners. We are committed to providing timely, affordable, and accessible financial and non-financial services tailored to the evolving needs of our members. Leveraging the expertise of our dedicated team and innovative technology, we strive to generate sustainable value for our shareholders, enhance member welfare, and foster long-term socio-economic growth within our communities.

Our Core Values

At SOM GLOBAL Multi-Purpose Cooperative Society, our core values are the guiding principles that shape our culture, drive our decisions, and define our commitment to excellence in serving our members and the broader community.

  • Genuineness of Purpose

    We are driven by a sincere and unwavering dedication to making a meaningful difference. Every initiative, decision, and action is rooted in our desire to uplift and empower our members, employees, and all stakeholders for long-term growth and shared prosperity.

  • Trustworthiness

    Trust is the bedrock of our cooperative. We conduct all engagements—internal and external—with honesty, openness, and transparency, fostering lasting relationships built on mutual respect and accountability.

  • Integrity

    We adhere to the highest ethical standards in all our dealings. Our commitment to doing what is right, even when no one is watching, ensures that we operate with responsibility, fairness, and respect for all.

  • Innovation

    We embrace change and welcome new ideas. Through creativity and adaptive thinking, we constantly explore smarter, more effective ways to serve our members, enhance our operations, and stay ahead in an evolving world.

  • Focus and Direction

    With a clear mission and a unified vision, we remain steadfast and purpose-driven. Our focused approach enables us to respond strategically to challenges, seize opportunities, and deliver sustainable value in every endeavor.
