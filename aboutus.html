About Us

Who We Are

SOM Global is a leading micro-finance institution dedicated to empowering individuals and businesses through innovative financial solutions. Our mission is to provide accessible and affordable financial services that foster growth and development in the communities we serve.

With a team of experienced professionals and a commitment to excellence, we strive to create lasting value for our customers, employees, and stakeholders. At SOM Global, we believe in building trust, fostering innovation, and delivering exceptional service.

Our Management

Our Vision

At SOM GLOBAL, we envision becoming Nigeria's leading micro-finance institution, renowned for delivering the highest level of customer satisfaction. We are dedicated to empowering low and middle-income earners through innovative and affordable financial services, aiming to create value that transcends expectations.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet profound: to alleviate poverty by providing timely, affordable, and accessible financial services to active low and middle-income earners. Through the expertise of our dedicated staff and the use of cutting-edge technology, we aim to deliver substantial returns to our shareholders while ensuring the satisfaction of all our stakeholders.

Our Core Values

  • Genuineness of Purpose

    Every decision and action at SOM GLOBAL is guided by a sincere commitment to positively impact the lives of our customers, employees, and stakeholders.

  • Trustworthiness

    We believe that trust is the foundation of every relationship. In every business transaction, we uphold honesty and transparency.

  • Integrity

    Our commitment to integrity ensures that we conduct business in a way that is ethical, responsible, and respectful to all our partners and customers.

  • Innovation

    As a forward-thinking organization, we continuously seek new ways to improve and deliver better services through innovative solutions.

  • Focus and Direction

    Our ability to stay focused on our goals and our mission has allowed us to navigate the ever-changing business landscape and provide lasting value to our stakeholders.
