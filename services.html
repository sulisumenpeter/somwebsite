Our Services

Innovative financial solutions designed for your success

SOM LOAN2SAVE

Daily Contributions

Empowering Traders with Accessible Financial Solutions

The SOM LOAN2SAVE Daily Contributions program is a flexible, short-term loan facility designed to support our cooperative members, particularly traders with daily or weekly incomes. This unique savings plan offers members the ability to access loans directly linked to their daily or weekly contributions, providing an efficient and reliable financial solution for those with consistent earnings.

SOM Young Shall Grow

Savings Account

Start Strong, Save Big for Tomorrow

Specifically tailored towards savings to meet the initial cost of settling and setting up business for young entrepreneurs after they have completed their training/apprenticeship. It is also an account for those who want to save towards starting up a small business that has the capacity of growing subsequently.

SOM Special Purpose

Savings Account

Secure Your Dreams with Purposeful Savings

With this product, individual customers save towards special occasions such as weddings, pilgrimage, property acquisition while corporate organizations could save gradually to finance end-of-year bonuses, replacement of fully depreciated assets, staff promotions, annual increments, etc.

Open Account

SOM Co-Operative

Savings Account

Achieve Goals Collectively

This account is designed to encourage group savings and to meet the needs of members who may require short-term credits to pay annual rent advance, children's school fees, acquire property, etc., without being asked to provide additional collateral.

SOM Global Subsidy

Subsidy & Empowerment Program

Financial Access for All

Empowering members with flexible savings and subsidy plans for education, housing, mobility, and business-offering up to 100% tuition or rent, vehicle support, and 300% loan value after consistent contributions.
